cookie policy

Your privacy is important to us. In order to improve our services and provide a more relevant experience to our users, we use "cookies." This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we use and how you can manage these cookies.

what are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by online service providers to facilitate and enhance your interaction with websites.

how we use cookies?

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site.

We use cookies from third-party partners, Google and Facebook, for analytics and marketing purposes. These cookies are used to track page views and visitor behavior which helps us to understand and analyze the online activity on our website. We use this information to enhance the functionality of our website and to provide better and more personalized content.

google analytics

We use Google cookies for analytics purposes. Google Analytics is a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. This data is shared with other Google services and they may use the collected data to contextualize and personalize the ads of their own advertising network.

For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/

calendly cookies and scheduling

Our website uses a scheduling tool provided by Calendly LLC. This tool uses cookies to track user interaction and facilitate the scheduling process.

what is calendly?

Calendly is an online appointment scheduling software that allows users to schedule meetings without the back-and-forth emails. It helps to organize your appointments in a simple, streamlined manner.

how we use calendly?

We use Calendly to provide a more convenient scheduling experience to our users. When you wish to schedule a meeting or appointment with us, Calendly collects information about your preferences for meeting times and dates. It also collects personally identifiable information such as your name and email address to facilitate the scheduling process.

calendly cookies

Calendly uses cookies to remember your preferences and enhance your scheduling experience. The cookies are used to help Calendly remember your information and settings when you view and use the scheduling tool.

your choices and opting-out

If you wish to opt out of the use of Calendly, you may choose not to schedule appointments using the tool. You can also manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings, but please note that this may impact the functionality of the Calendly service.

For more information on the privacy practices of Calendly, please visit the Calendly Privacy Policy web page: https://calendly.com/pages/privacy
